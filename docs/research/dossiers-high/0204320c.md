## SHA1 pitfall

**Why this recipe hits this mode:** `data_0219d9f0` is a named BSS pointer used as the load base; its pool .word is wildcarded by verify.py, so a wrong global address passes silently until ninja report. **Pool words to verify against orig .s:**
