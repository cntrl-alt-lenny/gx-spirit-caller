**Key lesson:** The `/5` divide must be on a signed `int` to emit `smull 0x66666667; asr #1` (not `umull`). The `v >>= 5` is a 64-bit logical-right-shift: the compiler emits `lsr r_lo, #5; orr r_lo, r_hi, lsl#27; lsr r_hi, #5`.
