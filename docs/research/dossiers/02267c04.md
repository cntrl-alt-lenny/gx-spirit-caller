The constant 0xCCCCCCCD is the ARM divide-by-5 magic (umull multiply for `/5`). The sequence:
- `umull r0,r1, r3, 0xCCCCCCCD` then `r1 >> 2` = `r3 / 5` (quotient)
- `r3 - (r3/5)*5` = `r3 % 5` (remainder) → slot index r9
- `eor r8, sl, r2>>2` = XOR of input arg with quotient → player index r8

The constant 0x1539 passed as `r2` to `func_ov002_021baca8` is a card type/filter code.

The packed arg to `func_ov002_021e30b4`:
- r0 = `(player_bit << 31) | 0x04200000 | (slot_idx_bits & 0x1F0000) | (id & 0xFFFF)`
- r1 = `(byte_field * 2 + flag) & 0xFFFF`
- r2 = 0
