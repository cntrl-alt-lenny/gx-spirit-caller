**Why similar:** Same ov016 MMIO RMW pattern: read DISPCNT twice (volatile), clear
enable bits, write back; then zero two scroll registers. The "two-step" structure
(register read-modify-write followed by a DMA-style clear) is directly analogous.
**Key lesson from that file:** Declare the target MMIO word as `volatile unsigned int *`
(or `vu32`). The two consecutive `ldr` in the asm are the signal — without volatile
mwcc CSEs the read. Write the clear of the scroll registers as separate assignments
in source order to get two `str` instructions in order.
