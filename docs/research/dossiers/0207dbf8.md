**Key lesson:** This is the align-up mirror of func_0207db8c. The critical differences are: (1) the alignment is UP not DOWN — `base = (cur + align-1) & mask` vs db8c's `newp = (cur - size) & mask`; (2) the fail compare is `end > h->hi` unsigned (movhi r0,#0) not `newp < h->lo` unsigned; (3) the return value is `base` (r5), not the aligned pointer — `cur` (r0) is only used as the Fill32 destination. The `span = end - cur` compute (`sub r2,r4,r0`) means `cur` must remain in r0 for the subtract, so do not rebind `cur` after the `Fill32` check.
