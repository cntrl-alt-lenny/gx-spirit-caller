**Why similar:** Uses `mla` (idx * 0x28) to compute a coord-slot address, then does a
bitfield RMW (`& mask | shifted_val`) on the result.
**Key lesson from that file:** Write the mla address as `base + idx * stride` directly in
the cast — `(unsigned int *)(data + idx * 0x28 + 0x8)`. The mask on the *shifted-val
side must be omitted* so mwcc can fuse the lsr into the orr: write
`(*p & MASK) | ((val << K) >> J)` not `(*p & MASK) | (val & FIELD_MASK)`.
