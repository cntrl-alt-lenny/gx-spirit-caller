## Recipe sketch

Arm 1 calls `021afb74` twice for two data pointers, extracts 8-bit slot bytes, calls `021e1870`, then sets flag to 10. Arm 5 does `rsb r0, r0, #1` (= `1 - bit0`) for opponent player, calls `0226b054`, clears `self->f8`, sets flag to 10. Arm 10 calls `021e286c`. Else returns 1.
