## Closest matched example
**Path:** src/overlay011/func_ov011_021cfaf8.c
**Why similar:** Immediately following address (021cfaa4 then 021cfaf8); same 021d4000 base; result of a bit-operation is bound and stored. The cfaf8 file shows the exact bind-then-store shape with a single expression binding.
**Key lesson from that file:** Bind the computed result in a single expression assigned to a local before any stores: `int result = (cond) ? a : b;`. Then store `result` to the target field — mwcc will allocate `result` to a callee-saved register that persists across the conditional and the store. If the ternary compiles to two conditional moves (moveq + movne), both arms must be different constants or different variables; a `v=B; if(c) v=A;` form produces `mov rD, #B` + `moveq rD, #A` (unconditional first, conditional second).
