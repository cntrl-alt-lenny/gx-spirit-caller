## Closest matched example

**Path:** src/main/func_02018fa0.c
**Why similar:** Both are short (60B class) functions that perform a single read-modify-write on a SystemWork offset using an arithmetic operation on the current value.
**Key lesson:** The two-branch form (explicit `if (sum > 0xffff) ... else ...`) is required to keep the stores as predicated `orrhi`/`orrls` rather than a single merged ternary store. Merging the two branches into a ternary `*p = (f & 0xffff0000) | (sum > 0xffff ? 0xffff : (sum<<16)>>16)` may emit one `orr` with a conditional source — different from the two predicated stores in the orig. The `(sum << 16) >> 16` in the else branch forces `lsl#16; lsr#16` for the low-16 extract (unsigned); the same peephole risk as func_02018e40 applies if mwcc substitutes `and #0xffff`.
